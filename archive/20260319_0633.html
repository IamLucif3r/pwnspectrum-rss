HybridPetya: UEFI-Powered Ransomware!
Researchers have discovered HybridPetya, a UEFI-based copycat of NotPetya, exploiting a vulnerability (CVE-2024-7344) to bypass Secure Boot. This allows attackers to install ransomware directly into the firmware – a devastating attack that's extremely difficult to detect and remove. The threat is significant.
Read more: https://www.welivesecurity.com/en/eset-research/introducing-hybridpetya-petya-notpetya-copycat-uefi-secure-boot-bypass/

A zero-day vulnerability in WinRAR is being weaponized by espionage groups to target high-value targets across Europe and Canada. Attackers are using spearphishing to trick users into installing the compromised software and then exfiltrating sensitive data. It's a classic, highly effective technique.
